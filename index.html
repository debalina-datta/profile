About Me

Welcome to my site!

I am a PhD student at the Department of Biosciences and Bioengineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, Maharashtra. Before moving to Mumbai, I have completed my Integrated Masters in Systems Biology from University of Hyderabad, Telegana, India

In the broadest sense, my research area of interest includes the amyloid formation of different proteins in different diseases. My doctoral research involves the study of tumor suppressor protein p53 - its amyloidogenicity and the consequent effect on the prognosis of cancer. This website contains an overview of my research experience, expertise and education.
If you have any questions, feel free to contact me.
